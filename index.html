After that, I searched for hidden elements within the animation and clicked on them to trigger movement and sound.

04

The most common action in my two-minute interaction was dragging the clock wheel to navigate between different points in time. My impression is that the primary goal of the interactive experience is to promote Richard Mille’s RM 07-01 Coloured Ceramics watch in a much more subtle and emotional way. While the ultimate aim is still to increase awareness and sales, Richard Mille uses this immersive “Endless Summer” journey to engage users through exploration, sound, and storytelling. This approach connects the luxurious brand with a chic lifestyle and builds a deeper emotional connection with potential customers, rather than relying on conventional advertising.

06

In my opinion, the interactive experience communicates its primary goal mainly through visuals and sound. Visually, it is structured around three different times of the day, and throughout these moments, it presents animations of familiar daily activities that are romanticised through a consistent colour palette. The use of saturated, playful colours strongly reflects the feeling of summer and helps create a cohesive mood across the experience.

In terms of sound, there is a background track that helps maintain this consistency of mood. However, when the user clicks and holds, additional sounds are revealed. These sounds are very familiar and relatable, similar to what you would hear in real life, which allows me to imagine myself within the scene just by listening.

The most frustrating part of the experience for me is searching for the hidden objects. In some scenes, I spent time clicking everywhere trying to find them, but it seems that this feature is only included in certain scenes and not others. The most satisfying part for me was noticing the small sounds that align closely with the illustrations. For example, in the tennis scene, there is a subtle tennis grunt when the players hit the ball, the sound of a plane passing by, and even the soft purring of a cat, even though it is only placed in a small corner of the animation. These details made me feel that the experience was very carefully considered and thoughtfully curated, and it allowed me to become fully immersed in the scene.
